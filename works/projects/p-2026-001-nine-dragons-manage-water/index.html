Nine Dragons Manage A Water

Information

Year: 2026

Medium: Defective sanitary ware, UV print on aluminum

Dimensions: Variable dimensions

The Chinese idiom “九龙治水” refers to jurisdictional overlap, where multiple regulatory bodies or government agencies hold concurrent authority over a single socio-economic or administrative domain. In this work, nine defective objects collected from a sanitary ware factory are monumentalized alongside nine signs of governing objectives in organizational management: development, production stability, safety, order, health, efficiency, standards, and responsibility. The work attempts to unpack the hidden grid of institutional control and bureaucratic inertia.
