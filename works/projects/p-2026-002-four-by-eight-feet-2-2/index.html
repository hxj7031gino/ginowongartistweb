Four by eight feet, 2/2

Information

Year: 2026

Medium: Engraving on student desks

Dimensions: 244 × 122 cm

The work attempts to expose a tension between the commodification of education and the practical value inherited from traditional Chinese education, where students are standardized within today’s education system and fed with meritocratic ideology while being distorted by edification. Here, 146 student desks are displaced and suspended, with renowned pedagogical aphorisms from Confucian culture engraved on their fronts, calling back the haunted, mandated morning recitations of the classroom.
